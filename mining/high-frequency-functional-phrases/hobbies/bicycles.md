# 100+ High-Frequency Functional Phrases — Bicycle Enthusiasts in Japan  
*(Safe, Practical & Community-Respectful)*

> 📌 **Legend**:  
> - 🚲 = Road/Commuting | 🏔️ = Gravel/Touring | 🔧 = Maintenance | 🤝 = Group/Community  
> - **[P]** = Polite (to strangers, shop staff)  
> - **[N]** = Neutral (cyclists, bike shop regulars)  
> - **[C]** = Casual (close cycling friends)  
> - 💡 = Pro tip | 🚫 = Avoid (rude/unsafe)
