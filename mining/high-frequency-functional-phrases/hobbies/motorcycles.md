# 100+ High-Frequency Functional Phrases — Motorcycle Enthusiasts in Japan  
*(Safe, Legal & Community-Respectful)*

> 📌 **Legend**:  
> - 🏍️ = General Riding | 🛠️ = Maintenance/Repairs | 🏬 = Dealership/Shopping  
> - 🗺️ = Touring/Travel | 🤝 = Group/Community | 🛑 = Safety/Legal  
> - **[P]** = Polite (to strangers, staff)  
> - **[N]** = Neutral (fellow riders, shop regulars)  
> - **[C]** = Casual (close friends only)  
> - 💡 = Pro tip | 🚫 = Avoid (unsafe/rude)
