> 📌 **Katakana Notes**:  
> - `*` = Katakana used in specific contexts (loanwords, tech, business, youth speech).  
> - **タイム** is *not* interchangeable with **時間** — only in compounds: ライフタイム, タイムアップ.  
> - **ディレイ**, **ウェイト**, **デッドライン** are widely understood in daily life (e.g., trains, apps, work).

> 🎯 **SOV Tip (for time expressions)**:  
> In Japanese, **time words usually come at the beginning or right before the verb**:  
> ✅ きょう は はちじ に でかけます。  
> *(Today, [I] will go out at 8 o’clock.)*  
> → `[Time] + は/に + [Subject] + [Object] + [Verb]`
