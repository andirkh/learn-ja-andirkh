### Yes/no :

- yes = hai, 
- no = iie, 

### Correct/incorrect :

- correct = sou desu
- incorrect = chigaimasu

### Positif/negatif :

- positif = desu
- negatif = dewa arimasen / jya arimasen

### Penunjuk :
- Kore, sore, are (ini/itu adalah)
- Kono, sono, sore (benda itu …)

### kata tanya :

Kata tanya pengganti (?): ka?

- wa (topic)
- no (kepemilikan)
- mo (juga)

Age 20, hatachi

Age: Use -sai
1 year old = issai
8 years old = hassai
10 years old = jussai
20 years old = hatachi (irregular!)

Days of the month: Irregular readings!
1st = tsuitachi
2nd = futsuka
3rd = mikka
...
10th = tōka
14th = jū yokka
20th = hatsuka

Ordinal numbers: Add dai- (第) for formal sequences
第一 = dai-ichi (first)
第三回 = dai-san-kai (third time/event)

Zero: Usually zero (ゼロ) or rei (零) in formal contexts (e.g., phone numbers: zero; temperature: rei-do)
